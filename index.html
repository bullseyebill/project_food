Taverna Mike

Our Story

Taverna Mikes' started in 1980 when Mike left Rhodes and came to Kos. In the early days tourism on the island was in it's infancy and it was 3 years later that the Taverna was built and opened. In the early days the Taverna was used as a venue for welcome drinks and dinners with the local hotels such as Club Escolette. Mike also branched out from the Taverna and organised and run jeep safaris

In 1991 Gina joined Mike at the Taverna and they both agreed that it was important to keep their guests satisfied and serve them in a relaxed but professional environment. To this day their ethos has paid off sucessfully as they have many returning guests all of whom they treat as close friends

Mike and Gina attach great importance to the quality of the food and drinks offered, Mike collects many herbs on the island himself and all of his fresh ingredients are locally sourced. Watch out for the homemade lemon liqeur - Limoncello! And finally after living together for 33 years Gina and Mike got married in 2018
